Disgaea: Hour of Darkness

The First of a Series

Disgaea Hour of Darkness is the first game in the series. It is a tactical rpg that was designed and published by Nippon Ichi Software. It was developed and released for the Playstation 2 console in 2003. Although, it has had multiple re-releases, which were retitled to Disgaea: Afternoon of Darkness, for the Playstation Portable in 2007, the Nintendo DS in 2008, and for Microsoft Windows in 2016.
